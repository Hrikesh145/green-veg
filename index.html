DO YOU KNOW?

What are the health benefits of eating vegetables?

Vegetables are rich in essential vitamins, minerals, and fiber, which help boost immunity, improve digestion, support heart health, and reduce the risk of chronic diseases like diabetes and obesity.

How should vegetables be stored to keep them fresh longer?

Leafy greens should be stored in the refrigerator in airtight containers or wrapped in paper towels. Root vegetables like potatoes and onions should be kept in a cool, dry place. Some vegetables, like tomatoes and cucumbers, should be stored at room temperature for better taste and texture.

What are the best ways to cook vegetables while retaining nutrients?

Steaming, roasting, and stir-frying are the best methods to retain nutrients in vegetables. Boiling can cause nutrient loss, so it’s best to use minimal water or consume the cooking liquid in soups and stews.
